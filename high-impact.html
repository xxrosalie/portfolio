Study Abroad in Prague

The first class I took when I studied abroad in Prague, was Astronomy. Prague was the perfect place to take this class because The Czech Republic had a lot of observatories that we could visit throughout our time there. My favorite project for this class was a presentation I made about exoplanets, which you can see here.

The second class I took in Prague was Food and Society. I thoroughly enjoyed this class because it gave me a chance to really learn about the food and culture of The Czech Republic. It also taught me so much about the history of food which I had never really thought about before.
