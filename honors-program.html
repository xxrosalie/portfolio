About The Honors Program

North Carolina State University's Honors Program recognizes exceptional students and allows them to take advantage of as much of what NC State has to offer, academically, personally, and professionally. The University Honors Program (UHP) provides a breadth and depth of unique educational experiences that engage academically-motivated students in transformative experiential learning. Students in the UHP excel at leadership, research, service, and global engagement.

Learn More!

Honors Seminars

The UHP offers honors seminars, which are small, graduate-style classes designed to expose students to how multiple disciplines approach real-world problems.

Honors Forum

The honors program offers honors forum, which includes guest lectures, arts and cultural events, and field trips to historic and cultural sites.

High Impact Experiences

Students complete atleast three high-impact experiences that show the students putting their knowledge into action.
